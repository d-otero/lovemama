WELCOME TO LOVE MAMA!

We are a NY-based Malay, Vietnamese and Thai influenced Southeast Asian Restaurant providing you tasty dinner in our Manhattan dining area. We also provide take out, and catering for individuals and groups or for private events.

With humble beginnings as a food cart, the popularity of LoveMama's Malaysian street food propelled to the opening a Manhattan restaurant in 2014.
